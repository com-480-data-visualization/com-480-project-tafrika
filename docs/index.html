Tafrika: Where data meets basketball

Welcome to Tafrika, your go-to platform for exploring the world of basketball through data visualization.

Our interactive platform interactive platform is designed to provide you with an immersive experience in exploring and analyzing NBA player performance through cutting-edge visualizations. Whether you're a coach, analyst, or basketball enthusiast, our tools will help you uncover hidden patterns, develop winning strategies, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.

Imagine being able to pinpoint the exact spots on the court where your favorite player excels, compare player efficiencies across the league, and identify who steps up in clutch moments. With our visualizations, you can dive deep into the game, beyond the surface stats, to truly understand player dynamics and performance trends. Let's take a closer look at what you can discover with our powerful tools.

Spatial Visualization

Step onto the court with our interactive scatter map, where you can visualize every shot taken by a player. This tool places you right in the action, showing you the precise locations and outcomes of shots on a basketball court diagram. Imagine exploring the shooting patterns of stars like Stephen Curry or LeBron James, seeing where they hit their sweet spots and where they struggle.

How to Use It:
  • Select the season, team, and player to visualize their shot data.
  • Apply filters to focus on specific shot types or areas.
  • For each player, you get a player card that displays player details, number of shots, and their accuracy for the corresponding area.
Insights You Can Gain:
  • Identify shooting hotspots and cold zones for individual players.
  • Develop targeted training drills and defensive strategies based on player tendencies.
